Newday On The Road

On February 12th 2016 Liberty Church Amsterdam is hosting Newday on the road at the Q-Factory in Amsterdam. The evening will feature Joel Virgo and Newday worship leader Sam Cox.

The event will start at 19:30pm and finish at 22:30pm (timing TBC). The event will be in English with live Dutch translation. Tickets are FREE but you'll need to reserve your space in advance.

Amsterdam Day

Come and spend the day with us and hear about the plans and vision for Liberty Church Amsterdam. They'll will be plenty of opportunity to pray and explore the city as well.

This day is open for anyone who wants to pray and support what we're doing and / or for people who might be considering joining our team and helping to plant this new church.
