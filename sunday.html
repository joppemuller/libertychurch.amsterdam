Sunday

Every Sunday we gather at 10:45am at the Mirror Center, right next to Amsterdam Muiderpoort Train Station. Catch the train, tram or park for free outside (street parking is free all day on Sundays).

The service normally lasts for around 90 minutes and will include a message from the Bible and a time of singing songs of worship. The service itself is in English and unfortunately we don't provide translation at this time.

There will also be separate activities for the children which is usually bilingual (Dutch & English).

Anyone is welcome to join us.
